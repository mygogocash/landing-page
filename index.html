Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I review my financial data?

We recommend reviewing your financial data at least once a week. Regular check-ins help you stay on top of your spending, track progress toward your goals, and make informed decisions.

What kind of financial data can I track with this app?

You can track a wide range of financial data, including income, expenses, budgets, savings, debt, and even investments. The app gives you a full picture of your financial health in one place.

Can I track my spending automatically?

Yes! You can connect your bank accounts and credit cards to the app to automatically import and categorize your transactions, making spending tracking effortless.

Does the app offer investment tracking?

Absolutely. You can link your investment accounts to monitor portfolio performance, asset allocation, and more—all in real time.

How do I sign up for the app?

Signing up is simple. Just download the app from the App Store or Google Play, follow the on-screen setup steps, and you'll be up and running in minutes.
